Individual Owners

You just had your vehicle struck by hail and are probably still processing what happened and what steps to take next. We have spent a lot of time to develop what we have called HAIL CONCIERGE. A FREE service when utilized will take all the headache out of getting your vehicle repaired. You will have a personal advisor working hand in hand on your behalf from start to finish. Including help filing a claim, writing a preliminary estimate, working with the insurance company to obtain the correct amount for repair, booking a rental, a tailored repair plan, and the one that everyone likes, deductible match. Honestly it’s as easy as giving us a call and booking your repair through HAIL CONCIERGE. We have made a few helpful videos that answer the most common questions we get. New for 2021 we have implemented a new repair process that will speed up your repair time by 50% getting you back into your vehicle quicker than before.
